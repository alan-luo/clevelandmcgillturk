Purpose of Study

Practice setting up a Turk study.

Task Description

You will be taken through an experiment in which you will be asked to judge values on some graphs. Afterwards, you will be asked to complete a demographic questionnaire.

You will be paid a base price of $0.00 and an additional bit of satisfaction for every correct response.
